RUNNER

Fast and aggressive.

Weakness: Can be taken out with basic weapons and headshots.

STALKER

Stealthy and rely on sound, they ambush their prey.

Weakness: Can be ambushed and taken out with stealth attacks.

CLICKER

Deaf but have sensitive hearing, they use echolocation.

Weakness: Can be killed with a headshot or a Shiv for a silent takedown.

BLOATER

Durable and use toxic spores.

Weakness: Vulnerable to fire and headshots.

SHAMBLER

Slow but powerful, they release toxic gas.

Weakness: Can be taken out with fire and headshots.

RAT KING

A large, heavily mutated infected with multiple tendrils.

Weakness: Vulnerable to fire and headshots, but requires multiple shots.
